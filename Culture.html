Some of the valuse of the uintah basin are: college, Continuing Education, and overall quality. There are also many different legends and superstitions in the basin one of the most popular being Skinwalker Ranch.

According to World Population Center as of 2023 the average family size is 3.65 and the average household size is 3.2.

In the forms of fun and leisure we have many things one of the biggest being the Uintah Recreation Center. Major industries of Utah mostly include: coal mining, cattle ranching, salt production, and government services.

According to World Population Center as of 2023, 91.91% of Uintah County, Utah residents speak only English, while 8.09% speak other languages. The non-English language spoken by the largest group is Spanish, which is spoken by 3.61% of the population.

Another major thing in the Uintah basin is Religion, in Uintah County, Utah. 75.3% of the people in Uintah County are religious: - 1.1% are Baptist - 1.3% are Episcopalian - 3.9% are Catholic.

In the Uintah basin there are a fair few options to colleges and other institutions including, Uintah Basin Technical Institute, Utah State University, and Uintah Basin Chistian acadamy.

One of the pides and joys of the Uintah Basin is our local shops especialy food such as 4 Brothers Pizza, Uintah Packing Co., Glazed & Grilled, and many more.
